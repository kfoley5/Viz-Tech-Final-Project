Insta Data

Welcome to insta data; an exploration of my Instagram discover feed and how the personal information Instagram collects translates to the content they show me.

This analysis was done by taking a screenshot of my discover feed every day for 28 days and categorizing the content into overarching themes and focusing on the three most prevelant: "Cooking", "Netflix", and "Film." I then downloaded data from Instagram to access their “Topics” list that they they have curated for me based on my activity.

Navigate the page by selecting a cateogory to see its prevelance on my discover feed, the percentage of my feed that the selected category took up, and the number of relevant Instagram "Topics." There are 28 empty frames represnting a screenshot of my discover feed on a given day. As you navigate through the categories, only the cells in which a picture from that category existed will be highlighted.
